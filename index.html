Welcome to Our Enterprise Systems Access Portal

This portal offers centralized access to all the resources needed for managing system access in enterprise system courses. From handling student data and course software requirements to maintaining operational playbooks and generating checklists, everything you need is in one place.

Students

The Student Management Feature simplifies roster handling with easy upload, viewing, comparison, and data export

Courses

The Course and Software Requirements Feature enables adding, viewing, and updating courses and their software needs

Playbooks

The Playbook Feature allows adding, viewing, and editing of operational playbooks for managing system access processes

Checklists

The Checklist Feature generates and downloads checklists to be sent to professors, detailing student access to specific software.
